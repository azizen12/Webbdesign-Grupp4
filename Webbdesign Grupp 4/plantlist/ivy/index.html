Ivy Hedera helix Ivalace

Hedera helix Ivalace is an attractive, evergreen, compact ivy. Its small, dark green leaves are glossy and have wavy (curled) edges. The leaves resemble kale in appearance. The stems are wine-colored. This variety is a true ornamental plant that looks perfect in a pot.
